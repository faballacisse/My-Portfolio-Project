Accountants are patient and attentive to details

5 August 2020 Blog

Accounting is a disciplined profession. You have to get the numbers right otherwise you risk misleading your stakeholders with false information.

Patience and attention to detail are practiced in each phase of accounting — from inputting the transactions to the accounting system, making adjustments, and creating financial reports.

Any accountant can relate to the experience of auditing your spreadsheet because numbers are not making sense — only to find out that you use the wrong cell reference in your formula.

The same is true with programming. Anyone with programming experience may relate to coding for hours but finding a bug when you run the program — only to find out that you use = instead of == somewhere in your code.

If you think about it — debugging is just the equivalent of auditing your spreadsheet — only it applies now to your script..
