All apps are just supercharged spreadsheets

4 Sept 2020 Blog

They are basically workarounds to the limitation of the traditional spreadsheets that we know of.

How do we use spreadsheets? We usually put data in them, manipulate them, and store the results.

But they have limitations — not so aesthetic, data can be corrupted, duplicate data, invalid data, not so secure, etc.

So we solve these limitations by separating data, logic, and presentation.

In other words, we accountants are one step away from creating or understanding any app as we have knowledge of the problems that are commonly solved by these apps.
